WHY I LIKE BLACKPINK

I first learned about Blackpink in December 2017 thanks to BTS. I was simply idly Googling when Blackpink appeared in the search suggestions. Curious, I checked it out and that's how I first heard "Stay," which has since become my favorite song. Their cool dancing moves and, of course, the bop-ness of their songs are why I like them. I also admire that their group is unique in that not every member is Korean. Rosé is Australian, Lisa is Thai, only Jennie and Jisoo are Korean.

INTERESTING FACT

    Blackpink, the most popular Korean girl group internationally, is constantly referred to as the "largest girl group in the world."

    Blackpink consists of 4 members; Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa and they debuted on August 8, 2016.

    Blackpink’s first release was the single album “Square One.” It included the songs “Boombayah” and “Whistle.” Whistle sold around 2,500,000 in South Korea alone.

    On June 26, 2020, Blackpink broke the record for the biggest premiere on YouTube. They had a total of 1.65 million fans who watched the unveiling of their new music video “How You Like That.”

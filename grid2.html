A FLOWER THAT SPEAKS TACENDA
Description:
"No letter that could be sent deserves to go undelivered."
─ Violet Evergarden

These are written in English, a collection of different poems that holds promising and relatable words. A flower that speaks tacenda, the things which are better to leave unsaid. In this world full of loquacious people, how long do I have to be silent to wait for my turn to speak, to be seen, and be heard?
❝ Tacenda — it means things better left unsaid. Sometimes, it's preferable to leave things unsaid than to speak it carelessly. One of the reasons is that we are afraid of what might be its consequences. Ergo, I chose to write it. Poets often write poems for certain persons in its mind. But no one can deny that once the reader understands each word of it, it is recognized as one of their own.❞

My collection of poems was once called "Shattered Heart" before becoming "A Flower That Speaks Tacenda".
A thousand thoughts I have in mind,

That is powerless to shout out loud.

Collection of poems that served as my voice,

That my mouth refuses to explain and became a silent noise.

Poems made by words of art;

And genuinely from the heart.
